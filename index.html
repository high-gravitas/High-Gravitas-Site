High Gravitas

Helping technology companies go from $0 to hypergrowth

What does High Gravitas do?

High Gravitas provides consulting, advising and investment to high-growth technology companies. Our focus, across consulting, advising and investing, is to help startups from company formation through first revenue and beyond with all things related to sales and go-to-market.

Why do we do it?

It's hard enough for founders to build scalable products, hire and retain talented employees and raise capital to deliver on a mission of solving hard problems. Layer on top finding product/market fit and scaling sales in an operationally efficient way and it's no wonder 90+% of all startups fail.

We've lived and breathed startups from company formation to raising multiple rounds of venture funding and scaling revenue from $0 to $10s of millions per year. Because we know how hard it is, helping companies that are finding product/market fit scale out their revenue efforts is incredibly gratifying.

How can we help?

We've helped startups across a range of areas in sales, marketing, partnerships and operations. Some examples include:

  • Implementation and development of initial sales processes and best practices.
  • Development of sales strategy including tactical elements such as pricing, presentation materials and enterprise deal structures.
  • Sales team construction and commission plans.
  • Hiring/recruiting of initial sales team members
  • Sales and marketing technology integration including CRM (Salesforce/Hubspot), Outreach (Mixmax/InsideSales), Prospecting (Clearbit/LeadIQ), Meeting management (Zoom and Gong/Chorus) and other tools to help teams scale operations.

High Gravitas is run by passionate startup operators and executives who love to enable new businesses to grow and flourish. If you're interested in learning more about how we help founders, contact us.
