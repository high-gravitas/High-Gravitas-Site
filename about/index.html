About High Gravitas

High Gravitas is operated by [Tim Geisenheimer](https://www.linkedin.com/in/timgeisenheimer/), a seasoned startup operator and executive. Tim has worked in leadership positions running Enterprise sales and BD teams across startups and large companies including Twitter, CBS and startups like TapCommerce and Timescale. At TapCommerce, Tim was a member of the founding team and helped to generate the first dollar of revenue all the way to 45 employees and an acquisition by Twitter for over $100 million. While at Twitter, Tim ran an Enterprise sales team that routinely beat revenue goals in excess of $100 million.
